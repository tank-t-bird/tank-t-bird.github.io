About

Realtime 3D artist and interactive developer based in Berlin, working at the intersection of spatial computing, motion design, and immersive technology.

With experience across Unreal Engine, Unity, WebGL, AR, XR, and VR — the work spans interactive installations, game advertising, museum exhibits, augmented reality apps, and realtime product visualization.

Former work includes clients such as Popcore, Shoutrlabs, Meshicon, IXDS for HERE, and Telekom Germany.
